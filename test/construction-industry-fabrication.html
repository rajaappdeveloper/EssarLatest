Structural Steel Fabrication Works

Essar Engineering Pte. Ltd. provides range of Steel Fabricated products that are quality tested as per local and international quality norms. Leveraging on high skilled workforce, Essar renders services using advanced tools and techniques under supervision of trained personnel. Keeping in mind cost effectivity, durability, and stable high-quality of steel construction solutions for the projects.

Since 2007, Essar Engineering Pte Ltd..’s Industrial and Facility Services Division has offered metal fabrication for high-rise commercial and industrial building constructions as part of its comprehensive services.

If you are in need of design services, engineering, furnished fabricated and installed parts, or on-site welding, Essar Engineering Pte. Ltd.’s Metal Fabrication and Machining shop has the resources and experience to assist you. Essar Engineering Pte Ltd generates design drawings using 2D & 3D CAD programs such as SolidWorks 2015 and AutoCAD 2017. Our expert team specializes in shearing, bending, cutting, forming, hole punching, notching and welding of sheets, plates, angles, channels, bars, pipes and beams.

Essar Engineering Pte Ltd. has completed numbers of custom fabricated projects ranging from general steel works to architectural and structural steel works including modifications and upgrades. We offer best quality services on your required finish for all types of materials such as mild steel, stainless steel, aluminium, copper etc.

Common projects include

  • Mezzanine Structures.
  • Ramp Rail Systems
  • Hoist Beams & Support
  • Utility Ladders
  • Stainless Steel Wall Panels
  • Spiral Stair and Rail Systems
  • Structural Steel Fabrication & Installation
  • Customized Gates (Manual & Motorized)
  • Stainless Steel Piping & Vessel Modifications
  • Miscellaneous supports for Pipes and Ducts
