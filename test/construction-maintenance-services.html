Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

We offer Annual Maintenance Contract or AMC to customers to ensure timely preventive maintenance. Essar Engineering Pte. Ltd., offers AMC with the ultimate goal to effectively and efficiently support the life cycle of the facility by eliminating unplanned shutdowns and realizing life-cycle cost savings.

The Annual Maintenance Contract ensures peace of mind of the client and involves minimal paperwork in getting service. AMC customer service calls are given priority over regular service calls ensuring minimal downtime and quicker redressal of complaints.
