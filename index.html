Who We Are

About Our Company

In the year 2007; an enthusiastic energetic professional engineer, post graduated from Singapore with vast experience in Projects handling, Fabrication and Construction industry after an initial period from General Electric (GE), has expanded this company from a Small machine shop to a Medium level Fabrication industry.

During these years, ESSAR acquired its BCA Registration (L1- Mechanical Engineering & GB2 - General Builders) with BizSafe 3 Certification. Now, ESSAR is going for ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001 and BizSafe Star Certification to enhance its competency and participation for major orders in the coming years.

We provide high quality fabrication works for Process and Construction Industry including On Site Erection and Commissioning Services.
