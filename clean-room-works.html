Professional Clean Room Solutions

Essar Engineering Pte. Ltd. provides clean and comfortable air conditioning and clean process environment that suit your requirement. We design, supply, build and install various types of clean rooms (Class 100, 1K, 10K, 100K) with strict adherence to GMP and FDA compliance.

Key Capabilities

  • Replacement of Ducts and Pipes.
  • Fabrication and installation of special Teflon coated SS 316 ducts for highly corrosive solvent exhaust gases.
  • Supervision, Erection and Commissioning of pipelines & systems.
  • Clean room configurations with return air recirculation modules.

Exhaust Ventilation and Ducting System

We offer the design and installation of General Exhaust Systems and Local Exhaust Systems for various industrial plants. Our designs follow professional calculations to maximize efficiency and provide an effective environment for users.
