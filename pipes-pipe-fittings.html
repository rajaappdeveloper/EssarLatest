Industrial Pipe Fittings

Essar Engineering Pte. Ltd. offers pipe fittings in a variety of configurations suitable for a broad range of industrial applications.

We manufacture pipes and fittings with pressure ratings optimized for various temperatures, ensuring they stand up during prolonged exposure to extreme conditions. Our fittings are constructed using standard materials like 316 Stainless Steel, Brass, and Carbon Steel, with options for specialty alloys.
